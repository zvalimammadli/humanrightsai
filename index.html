About the Summit

The Baku Ombudsmen Summit 2026 brings together Ombudsman institutions, National Human Rights Institutions, and international experts to explore the opportunities, risks and responsibilities of artificial intelligence in the context of human rights.

Key Themes

• AI and human rights protection
• Ethical and responsible AI
• Digital transformation and justice
• The role of Ombudsman institutions
