About the Summit

The International Baku Summit of Ombudsmen will bring together Ombudsman institutions, National Human Rights Institutions, international organizations, public authorities, civil society representatives and leading experts to discuss the impact of Artificial Intelligence on human rights and democratic governance.

Summit Introduction Video

Main Themes

AI and Human Rights

Exploring the opportunities and challenges of artificial intelligence for human rights protection.

Privacy & Data Protection

Addressing privacy concerns, surveillance technologies and responsible data governance.

Non-Discrimination

Tackling algorithmic bias and promoting equality in the digital environment.

Role of Ombudsmen

Strengthening the role of Ombudsman institutions and NHRIs in monitoring AI systems.
