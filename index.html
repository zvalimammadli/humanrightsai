About the Summit

The International Baku Summit of Ombudsmen serves as an international platform for discussing the opportunities, risks and responsibilities arising from the development of artificial intelligence, while promoting approaches grounded in human rights principles.

Date: 18–19 June 2026
Venue: JW Marriott Absheron Baku
Location: Baku, Azerbaijan

Baku Declaration

The Baku Declaration on Artificial Intelligence and Human Rights will reflect the key outcomes of the International Baku Summit of Ombudsmen.

The document will be made available after its adoption by the participants of the Summit.
