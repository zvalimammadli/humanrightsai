About the Summit

The Baku Ombudsmen Summit serves as an international platform for discussing the opportunities, risks and responsibilities arising from the development of artificial intelligence, while promoting approaches grounded in human rights principles.

Date: 18–19 June 2026
Venue: JW Marriott Absheron Baku
Location: Baku, Azerbaijan
